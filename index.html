🚀 Research & Projects

GMRT Radio Astronomy Research

Conducting cutting-edge research on fringe stopping and fractional delay correction for the Giant Metrewave Radio Telescope using advanced lookup table algorithms. This research project is part of the NIUS inititaive undertaken by the Homi Bhabha Centre for Science Education.
October 2024- January 2026

CUDA-Accelerated Exoplanet Detection

Developed a high-performance K-Nearest Neighbors algorithm using CUDA for GPU acceleration to detect exoplanets in astronomical datasets. Achieved significant speedup and accuracy in processing large-scale astronomical data.

Repository

Age of Universe Prediction Model

Created a comprehensive Jupyter notebook implementing the Lambda-CDM cosmological model to predict the age of the universe. Integrated various cosmological parameters and observational data for accurate predictions.
